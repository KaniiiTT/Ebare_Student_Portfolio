    Protect Your Data

    At NetAware, we help you understand the difference between data privacy and data protection. According to Cloudian. (2025, November 6), Data privacy determines who can access your personal information, while data protection uses tools and policies to secure it. Learn practical steps to safeguard your personal health information (PHI) and personally identifiable information (PII), prevent breaches, and maintain control over your data in today’s digital world.

    Steps to Protect Your Data

    1. Use Strong Passwords

    Create long, unique passwords that mix letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using your birthday or name.

    Learn More

    2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

    Add an extra layer of security by using two-factor authentication (2FA) for your online accounts.

    Learn More

    3. Keep Software Updated

    Regular updates fix security flaws and protect you from the latest online threats.

    Learn More

    4. Watch Out for Phishing

    Don’t click on suspicious links or emails asking for personal information.

    Learn More

    5. Backup Your Data

    Regularly back up important files to an external drive or cloud storage to avoid losing them.
